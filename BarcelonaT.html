Barcelona: Transnational meeting

A good start to our international project. November 2017

This transnational meeting in Barcelona starts the Erasmus + Project From Me to We, a joint project of schools from Germany, Italy, Poland, Spain, Finland and Croatia. The main goal of the project is support students’ self-esteem and increase their self-knowledge, reinforce collaboration and cooperation in teaching and learning and make school environment a place to foster creativity and create emotions.

Aims

Our project focuses on individuals and showing them that dealing with problems as part of a network of people will lead to easier, faster and better results. We want to help teachers as well as students to strengthen their self-esteem. We want to show that emotional stability will increase motivation in tackling demanding tasks in school. This applies to both teachers and students. At a basic level it means being able to ask for help and being willing to give it. The activities are designed to promote teamwork while at the same time working on topics closely related to empowerment, self-esteem and solidarity. We would like to move from an impact at the individual level, to an awareness of the importance of solidarity at increasingly more encompassing levels such as the school, the job market, the national level and finally the European Union.

Work process

There will be three kinds of project activities: 1) the ones that will take place in transnational teacher-student meetings, 2) the ones that will happen in each school before and after the teacher-student meetings and 3) the ones that will be done before and during the coordinator-teacher/principal meetings. As part of each activity students and teachers will work on writing a blog post about the activity to involve the whole school community in the project. Video-conferences will be organized on a regular basis. Each action will be held regularly according to the following modules: Who we are Our website For each transnational mobility - goals - Activities - community building - Tools for documentation Travel schedule Tasks pre and post mobility Dissemination
