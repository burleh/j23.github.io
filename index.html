Learning to be global citizens

CE JOAN XXIII welcomes exchange students from its partner institutions around Europe. We have projects with institutions across Europe. CE JOAN XXIII offers a wide range of stimulating and valuable exchange programmes to students at partner institutions. We are open to initiate collaboration with schools and other organizations around Europe and beyond.
