Cultural Exchange

This project involves 15 year-old students and deals with the different identities between our countries. An amazing experience for students who want to live with foreign families for a week. They also understand to be open-minded and learn to be more autonomous by doing activities and collaborating with foreign students. It's always unforgettable to enjoy a new country with wonderful people.
